Safety Measures During COVID

COVID-19 has disrupted our daily lives as we know it. Whether you own a newly converted childcare center, well-established center or an overflow center, we have created a safety checklist to help ensure the safety of your children, their parents, your staff and your school. We provide guides on security and infrastructure for your facility, physical safety measures, and emergency and general safety measures. The safety checklist provides a list of measures to enact for your school under the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Benefits Of Early Bilingual Education

Speaking two or more languages has obvious practical benfits in an increasingly globalized world. In recent years, however, research has shown that being bilingual helps us develop in other important ways. It has a profound effect on the improving cognitive skills not related to language, stimulating creativity, and even shielding against dementia in old age.
